Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

Patch 7.0.0 Notes

    Patch notes are provided by the development team behind Smash Ultimate with every update to the software. These updates often change the current meta of the game, causing huge shifts in the competitive scene. The 7.0.0 patch marked the end of the first DLC (downloadable content) wave, and by extension the first "book" of this game's life.

    In this particular patch, many characters received buffs to their shields. The characters whose shields are now larger are:

      DK, Link, Samus, Dark Samus, Yoshi, Kirby, Pikachu, Captain Falcon, Bowser, Ice Climber, Sheik, Zelda, Pichu, Falco, Young Link, Ganondorf, Mewtwo, Mr. Game & Watch, Meta Knight, Pit, Dark Pit, Zero Suit Samus, Snake, Squirtle, Charizard, Sonic, King Dedede, Olimar, Lucario, Wolf, Villager, Wii Fit Trainer, Rosalina & Luma, Little Mac, Greninja, Mii Swordfighter, Palutena, PAC-MAN, Bowser Jr, Duck Hunt, Ryu, Ken, Cloud, Corrin, Bayonetta, King K. Rool.
